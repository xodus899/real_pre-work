Pontiac G8

Ranging from 400 to 600 horsepower.These are low 13 second stock in the 1/4 mile.With minor modifications they have become bottom 11 second 1/4 mile cars.

Minor modifications to the G8 include:

  • Built by me, not Bought
  • Vararam Intake
  • 1 3/4" Long tube headers
  • 2.5" Exhaust
  • Underdrive Crank Pulley
  • Ported LS3 intake manifold
  • Custom grind 221/232 111lsa cam
  • Dry single fogger nitrous kit
  • 3000 PI torque converter
  • Custom tuned using Hptuners

Resulting in a 1.59 60' 11.64 1/4 mile @ 116 mph +1800 DA on a 70 shot.Correcting to 11.2 1/4 mile in desirable air.
